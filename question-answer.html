04 : Q&A

What is the history of Digital Nachos?

As a designer and web developer, Aneta knows that many companies need skilled IT professionals and there aren’t plenty in the Netherlands. With Annette’s experience in recruitment, we noticed the opportunity of matching IT vacancies with tech specialists, but not the usual way. We have big hearts for society and our drive is to contribute to a new way of business and a fairer world.

What value do we create?

We always take the time for an in-depth interview, to make sure we know exactly what you want. Yes, we can do so, because we work in IT ourselves. We guarantee quality and expertise. Our approach is friendly and informal. Apart from professional value, we want to be an example for doing business in a different way. Read more in our Mission section.

What can you expect from us?

Are you actively or passively looking for a new IT challenge, or your next IT hero to lift your business to a higher level? We are happy to get in touch. We will invite you for a digital intake interview and discuss the possibilities. Do we find your match? We then arrange an interview with the company, or two, or three. When all is positive and everyone’s excited, our job is done. When you have started in your job, of course we’d love you to keep us updated!
