03 : About us

We believe everyone is born into this world with exceptional talent. To discover, use and develop talent brings satisfaction and happiness. We are grateful to facilitate your new adventure and make your talent thrive. So that you can do what makes you happy.

We believe everyone deserves a shot at luck. Regardless of social status and financial resources. Digital Nachos donates 50% of its profit to a Dutch organisation that offers children the opportunity to develop skills such as respect, resilience and working together. To make them feel like heroes.

We believe the world needs a new business system. Where the objective is to create value for others and make a living out of that. We have taken enough. Now it’s time to give back.

This is not something extraordinary. We’re just lucky to be privileged.
